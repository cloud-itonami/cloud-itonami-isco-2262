## Customer

- patients
- clinics
- long-term care facilities

## Offer

- prescription verification
- dispensing
- patient counseling
- interaction checking
- reporting

## Revenue

- per-fill fee
- monthly practice platform fee
- counseling fee

## Trust Controls

- no controlled-substance dispensing without governor gate
- dosage changes always require pharmacist sign-off
- dispensing records are auditable, not editable
